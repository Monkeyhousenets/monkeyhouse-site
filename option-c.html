What's a Treenet?

A handwoven world between your trees

A treenet is a completely handwoven structure made from rated climbing materials. Every single one is designed around your specific trees — because the tree tells you what's possible in the space.

It's going to stand the test of time and be a lot of fun for the entire family — every single age that ever wants to play on it.

💪

Stronger Than the Trees

Every component UIAA-rated. When it's all tied together, it's probably stronger than the trees themselves.

🌳

Tree-Safe Hardware

Spacer blocks protect the bark. Your trees keep growing healthy while you play.

✍

Entirely Handwoven

No prefab panels. Every knot tied by hand, on-site, in your trees.

🎨

Completely Custom

Custom paracord colors and weave patterns. No two nets look the same.

The Builder

Built by hand, with love for the craft

Tim is a highliner, rope nerd, and the person behind every Monkeyhouse treenet. He weaves every net by hand, on-site, working with the trees to create something that's completely unique to your space.

Every detail matters — from the hardware that protects your trees to the paracord patterns that catch the light. These aren't products. They're handmade places to play, lounge, and feel wonder.

"Every treenet is so much fun to build because it's dependent on the trees — you work with the tree and the tree tells you what's possible."
