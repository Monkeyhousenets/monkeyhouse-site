What's a Treenet?

There's something that inspires wonder in people when they see one of these things.

A treenet is a completely handwoven structure made out of rated climbing materials — extremely strong, really bouncy or loungy depending on the tension. Every single one is built to work with your specific trees, because the tree tells you what's possible in the space.

Every component is rated by the UIAA. When you tie absolutely everything together and every bit of weight is distributed, it's probably stronger than the trees themselves.

"It's bouncy like a trampoline, loungy like a hammock. It fits in the environment and it just feels like you're at home in a tree."
