What's a Treenet?

A handwoven world between your trees

Picture a giant net woven from climbing-grade rope, strung between the trees in your backyard. You can bounce on it, nap in it, do backflips off the walls — and it's all built by hand, on-site, shaped around whatever your trees make possible.

Every piece of hardware is rated for climbing. The whole structure is engineered to hold — as solid as the oaks it hangs from.

"It's bouncy like a trampoline, loungy like a hammock. It fits in the environment and it just feels like you're at home in a tree."
